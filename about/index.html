OUR STORY

Building the infrastructure
for industrial decarbonization

Avalanche Consulting and Management Services, LLC was founded to close the critical gap between industrial operations and carbon markets — empowering manufacturers to turn compliance into competitive advantage.

OUR MISSION

Accelerate the climate economy by equipping industry with precision tools and market access.

We believe that sustainability is not an extra-credit activity — it's a data-driven, value-generating aspect of running a successful business. Our mission is to make decarbonization simple, fast, and profitable for manufacturers across the US and Africa.

OUR VISION

A world where every industrial facility is a node in a transparent, efficient carbon economy.

By 2036, we envision CarbonScope360 as the recognized standard for industrial carbon accounting — connecting thousands of facilities to global carbon markets and driving 100+ million metric tons of verified reductions.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Core values that guide every decision
